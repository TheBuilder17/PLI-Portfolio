About Me

I design for the humans behind the screen.

I'm Oluwatobi Oyesola — a Product Designer with a deep belief that the best interfaces disappear. They stop being interfaces and become experiences people simply live inside.

My work sits at the intersection of design thinking and user psychology. I thrive on complexity — taking tangled problems and returning clarity. Whether I'm mapping user journeys or polishing final pixels, I hold both the big picture and the fine details.

I've designed for startups finding their footing, and for established brands redefining how they show up digitally. Every project earns the same attention.

Design Philosophy
"Good design doesn't shout — it guides. It earns trust before it earns attention."
01
Empathy First

Every screen represents a real person with real goals. I start with understanding before I start designing.

02
Systems Thinking

Great design scales. I build with components, patterns, and principles so that every new screen inherits the work done before it.

03
Impact by Clarity

Complexity is the enemy of adoption. My measure of success is not how clever the design is, but how effortlessly it works.

How I Work

A process built on rigour and craft.

I don't design by instinct alone. Every decision is traceable — back to a user insight, a business goal, or a validated assumption. This is what separates decoration from design.

01

Discover & Define

Before anything is sketched, I listen. Stakeholder interviews, user research, competitive audits — this phase transforms vague briefs into sharp problem statements.

User Interviews Competitive Analysis Problem Framing
02

Ideate & Architect

With a clear problem, I explore the full solution space. Information architecture, user flows, and low-fidelity wireframes that test logic before investing in pixels.

User Flows Wireframing IA Mapping
03

Design & Refine

High-fidelity design built on a robust component system. Typography, colour, spacing, motion — every detail calibrated for the user and the brand in equal measure.

Design Systems Visual Design Interaction Design
04

Test & Ship

Usability testing with real users, iteration on findings, then handoff with thorough documentation. I stay close through development — because design doesn't end at delivery.

Usability Testing Prototype Validation Dev Handoff
